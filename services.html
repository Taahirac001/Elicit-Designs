Web Design Process

Strategy

Before we design the site, we will interview and discuss with you deeply about your business. We will ask many questions about your goals, values, target audience, and more. We study your business so that we can create a realistic timeline and a detailed project plan that will exceed your expectations.

UX/UI

After Discussing your business and formulating a strategy, we move to the next step and begin working the User Exerience (UX/UI). Inorder to create a memoreable user experience, we focus on simplicity, usability, and functionality while removing any known complications. This requires in depth research and testing for our UX/UI team. functionalities, website layout, usability, and features are mapped out in a wireframe, which is a blueprint of the website.

Mockup

Once the wireframe has been created, reviewed, and decided on, we send it the web designer who will then create the mockup. This is where the aesthetics of the webite are applied. Use of color, fonts, banners, graphic designs, and interactions with elements are done in the mockup. The mockup is what your website will look like in it's final form prior to development.

Web Development Process

Implementing Designs

Once the developers recieves the mockup from the design team, we begin building the website. The mockup is a step by step guide for the developers in building the website. This way, we can prevent wasting time on hard work in coding a website that was not fully planned out.

Front-end coding

All front-end coding is done with HTML5 and CSS3 while following best practices. Coding the front-end side consist of building out the layout, the colors, font, and other aesthetic features. It also includes building out the flow and usuability of the website. Now that more searches on Google are done via mobile phone, we make every single website responsive.

Back-end Coding

Back-end coding can actually be done in tandem with front-end coding. PHP, Drupal, Python, JavaScript, jQuery, and more are used. The back-end development consist of making sure that the website is functional. From contact forms submissions to page loading time, your website should flow seemlessly.

E-commerce Process

E-commerce Strategy

Starting from crafting a compresenive approach to your e-commerce strategy, we discuss your goals and values, the webdesign process, content, and development. In E-commerce we dive into your target audience needs, exceptional selling propositions, and call to actions. The goal is to create a e-commerce strategy that beat out your competition and increase sales.

Customers First

Making your website with the customer in mind will increase your sales. However, your goals and values are also important. Your website is your personal sales person that works 24/7. There are many ways in which we use your goals and the customers needs to get your website to close deals. Such as having filtering options on your category pages, suggesting pages that will help send traffic to specific products, and providing enough product details so that the customer feels confident in their purchase.
